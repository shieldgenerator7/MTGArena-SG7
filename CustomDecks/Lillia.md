Lillia Dream MTG Deck

This is different from the previous idea, because that one was based on replicating Lillia’s gameplay from League of Legends in Magic The Gathering. But this idea is more about Lillia’s character and lore.

Concept: Cards in hand are dreams, and Lillia gets stronger when people’s dreams are stronger.

Win condition: Make opponents draw lots of cards, then one shot them with massive damage that scales with the number of cards they have in hand.

Some of the following text was copied from notes I took in Google Keep on the bus, and then modified.

lillia legendary  
4 cost, 0/1  
lillia gains +X/+X and Ward X, where X is the biggest hand size among your opponents.   
tap: target opponent draws 1 card  
opponents have no hand size limit

watch out! eep!  
sorcery, 2 cost   
deal X damage to target opponent, where X is equal to their hand size. If they're Sleeping, deal an additional Y damage to them, where Y is equal to your hand size

lilting lullaby: sorcery 3 cost  
all opponents with hand size 8 or more are now Sleeping (Sleeping players can't play cards from their hand, and lose Sleeping if they take damage)

blooming blows: 1 cost instant  
choose target opponent. deal 1 damage to up to X creatures that opponent controls, where X is their hand size

shared dream: 2 cost enchantment  
whenever you draw a card, each opponent draws 1 card

bashful bloom: 2 cost enchantment  
at the start of your turn, if an opponents hand size is greater than your life total, gain 1 life.  
whenever you take damage, target opponent discards 1 card.

Dreaming Tree  
2 cost, 0/5  
enchantment plant creature  
when Dreaming Tree enters the battlefield, you may search your library for an elk or centaur creature, reveal it, put it in your hand, and then shuffle your library.  
When Dreaming Tree is destroyed, you may play an elk or centaur creature from your hand or command zone without paying its mana cost.  
If this card is in your starting hand, you may start the game with it without paying its mana cost.

Dream Journal  
2 cost artifact  
Whenever an opponent draws a card outside their draw step, you may return an instant or sorcery card from your graveyard to your hand.

Lillia, Dream Realizer  
5 cost, 3/4  
enchantment elk centaur creature  
Lands opponents control have “Return this land to your hand: Gain 1 mana of any color. Activate only during your main phase.”

Dream-Laden Bough  
3 cost equipment  
Equip creature 1  
1 mana, Tap: Choose target player. Deal 1 damage to X creatures that player controls, where X is that player’s hand size.

Swirlseed  
1 cost instant  
Target opponent Scrys X, where X is their hand size. Then they draw 1 card. If it is a creature card, they draw 2 more cards.

Bonk!  
2 cost instant  
Deal X damage to target creature, where X is that creature’s controller’s hand size. If that creature is tapped, increase the damage by your hand size. Excess damage is dealt to that creature’s controller.

Lillia, Strong Capable Dream Fawn  
5 cost, 5/5  
Trample  
Whenever an opponent draws a card outside their draw step, Lillia does 1 damage to them.

The Death of the Dreaming Tree  
2 cost Saga  
1\. Reveal the top X cards of your deck, put one of the revealed cards in your hand, then put the rest on the bottom of your library in any order, where X is the hand size of target opponent.  
2\. Gain 2 mana of any color combination. Use this only to play a plant or enchantment creature from your hand.  
3\. Tap target creature with toughness greater than its power. It doesn’t untap on its controller’s next untap step.  
4\. Destroy target 2 cost artifact or enchantment.

Root Infection  
1 cost instant  
Destroy target artifact or enchantment with converted mana cost X or less, where X is that artifact’s or enchantment’s controller’s hand size.

Chop the Tree  
2 cost instant  
Deal 2 damage to target creature. Then if it’s a plant, artifact, or enchantment, destroy it.

Thought Cloud  
3 mana sorcery  
Target opponent reveals their hand until the end of their next turn. During this time, each player may 2 mana as an interrupt. If they do, they choose a revealed card. That card’s controller then discards that card and draws 1 card.

# Lillia Dream MTG Deck

Concept: Cards in hand are dreams, and Lillia gets stronger when opponents’ dreams are stronger.

Win condition: Make opponents draw lots of cards, then one shot them with massive damage that scales with the number of cards they have in hand.

## Sleeping emblem:

“You can’t play cards from your hand. The next time you take damage, take additional damage equal to your hand size and destroy this emblem. Whenever you discard a card, if you have hand size of 7 or less, destroy this emblem.”

Lillia, Bashful Bloom  
4 cost, 0/1  
Legendary Enchantment Elk Centaur Creature  
lillia gains +X/+X and Ward X, where X is the biggest hand size among your opponents.   
tap: target opponent draws 1 card  
opponents have no hand size limit

Watch out! Eep!  
sorcery, 2 cost   
Target opponent draws 3 cards. Deal 1 damage to that opponent.

Blooming Blows  
sorcery, 2 cost  
Each opponent draws 1 card and takes 1 damage.

Lilting Lullaby  
sorcery 3 cost  
all opponents with hand size 7 or more gets a Sleeping emblem (“You can’t play cards from your hand. The next time you take damage, take additional damage equal to your hand size and destroy this emblem. Whenever you discard a card, if you have hand size of 7 or less, destroy this emblem.”)

Fawn Mower  
1 cost instant  
Target opponent draws 1 card. deal 1 damage to up to X creatures that opponent controls, where X is their hand size.

Shared Dream  
2 cost enchantment  
You draw a card during each player’s draw step.  
Whenever you draw a card outside a draw step, each opponent draws 1 card.

Bashful Bloom  
2 cost enchantment  
at the start of your turn, if an opponents hand size is greater than your life total, gain 1 life.  
whenever you take damage, target opponent discards 1 card.

Dreaming Tree  
2 cost, 0/5  
enchantment plant creature  
when Dreaming Tree enters the battlefield, you may search your library for an elk or centaur creature, reveal it, put it in your hand, and then shuffle your library.  
When Dreaming Tree is destroyed, you may play an elk or centaur creature from your hand or command zone without paying its mana cost.  
If this card is in your starting hand, you may start the game with it without paying its mana cost.

Dream Journal  
2 cost artifact  
Whenever an opponent draws a card outside their draw step, you may return an instant or sorcery card from your graveyard to your hand.

Lillia, Dream Realizer  
5 cost, 3/4  
legendary enchantment elk centaur creature

Dream-Laden Bough  
3 cost equipment

Swirlseed  
1 cost instant  
Target opponent Scrys X, where X is their hand size. Then they draw 1 card. If it is a creature card, they draw 2 more cards.

Bonk!  
2 cost instant
